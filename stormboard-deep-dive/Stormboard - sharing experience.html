Share & invite dialog

The "Invite Users" button at the bottom of a Storm opens the share modal. It exposes three tabs: Invite via Link, Invite via ID/Key, and Invite via Email.

Permissions & roles

Stormboard separates Team roles (Member vs Guest — billing distinction) from Storm roles (Administrator, Contributor, Viewer, Guest Contributor — per-Storm permission level).

Public link / external share

Stormboard does not publish a true public/anonymous URL — external sharing flows through email-invited Guest accounts. Guest Pass (Enterprise) is how a Guest temporarily gets contributor rights in a single Storm.

Export & download

Stormboard exports through its Reports feature — 12+ formats, including Microsoft Office (Word/PPT/Excel), Google Workspace (Docs/Slides/Sheets), high-res Storm screenshot, word cloud, and raw data (CSV/JSON/XML/OPML).
